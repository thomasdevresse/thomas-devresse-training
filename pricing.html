  • Two 30-minute calls each month, covering training, nutrition and lifestyle

Same commitment as in person: 12 weeks to start, then 4-week blocks.

Recommended start

Build the foundation first.

To get the most from Online, I recommend starting with an in-person assessment—ideally completing your first 12-week phase in person—so techniques and principles are solid before going fully remote.

Online suits self-driven people with some training experience, a gym they like, and a schedule or location that makes weekly in-person coaching impractical.

Fully remote and can’t make it to Brussels? Say so in your application—we plan the assessment and the first weeks around the distance.

How to choose

Choose a format, not a package.

Train in person if you can—nothing replaces coached practice on the gym floor. Twice a week while you are learning or chasing an ambitious change; once a week for steady, long-term progression.

Choose Online when distance or schedule rules out weekly in-person sessions—the same system, followed remotely.

You do not need to decide alone. The application lets me understand your goal and constraints, and the free assessment settles the rest together.
