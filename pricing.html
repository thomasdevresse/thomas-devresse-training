Coaching & pricing

Transformation. With the right amount of support.

Three formats, three different jobs. Transformation is the intensive route for significant physique change. Essential and Online support ongoing progress, strength, hypertrophy and better training.

Pricing is shown openly. The assessment and qualification process determine which format—and, for Transformation, which in-person cadence—makes sense.

01 · Six-month intensive

Transformation

For committed people who want substantial, measurable physique change and need a complete structure around training, nutrition and habits.

A 60–90 minute assessment establishes your starting point. From there, the number of in-person sessions varies with your needs, schedule and budget—the coaching framework remains the same.

Included in every Transformation cadence

  • 60–90 minute movement, strength, habits and objective assessment
  • Personalised six-month roadmap and training programme
  • Weekly programme review and coaching-app tracking
  • One grouped video-feedback submission each week
  • 30-minute progress review every two weeks, covering training, nutrition, habits and progress data as relevant
  • Documented measurements and progress photos at agreed checkpoints
  • Habit coaching and practical nutrition education within professional scope
  • Formal month-five decision: graduate, continue or extend

The monthly amount is an instalment of the agreed six-month programme, not a month-to-month subscription. Six months is the initial structured phase; longer-term goals may continue into a second phase after the month-five review. The recommended session envelope and total investment are confirmed before you commit.

How to choose

Start with the outcome, not the package.

Choose Transformation when physique change requires coordinated work across training, nutrition and habits. Choose Essential or Online when the main need is better training, programming, technique or performance.

You do not need to decide alone. The application lets me understand the goal, the support you need and whether I am the right coach for it.
