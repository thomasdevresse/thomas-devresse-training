Questions

Good to know

Why is the price shown per 4 weeks?

+

Training rhythm runs in 4-week blocks, not calendar months. Pricing per 4 weeks keeps it honest — you pay for the sessions you actually get. Essential is billed in 4-week blocks; Signature and Integral show the per-4-weeks figure and the full program total.

What are the cancellation & rescheduling rules?

+

The same rule applies across every formula: a session can be rescheduled if you let me know at least 24 hours in advance. Cancelled or moved with less than 24 hours' notice, the session is counted. This keeps the schedule fair for everyone — and it's exactly the kind of consistency the method is built on.

Why is Kaizen Integral by application?

+

It's a high-touch, piloted program with only 2–3 spots per quarter, so I can carry the load properly for each client. A short interview makes sure the fit and the goal are right before we commit.
