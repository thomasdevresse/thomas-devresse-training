My philosophy

改善 — small steps, endlessly repeated

Kaizen is a Japanese philosophy rooted in one idea: it's not about perfection — it's about progress. The goal is never the destination; it's the process of constantly becoming better. Through continuous cycles of small, deliberate improvements, you assess where you are, act on what can be better, and repeat.

This philosophy is the anchor of everything I do as a coach. Every decision — load, set, meal — is grounded in the scientific literature and in your real data, not in trends. Nothing is rushed. Nothing is skipped.
